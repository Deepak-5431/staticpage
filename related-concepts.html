Indian Geography
The Vindhya and Satpura ranges are important mountain ranges in central India that separate the northern plains from the Deccan plateau.
River Systems
Major rivers in India flow through various mountain ranges and valleys, creating distinct geographical features and ecosystems.
Narmada River
The Narmada River flows westward through the rift valley between the Vindhya and Satpura ranges, making it unique among Indian rivers.
